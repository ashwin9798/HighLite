Create stunning highlight reels on the fly.
It's fuckin magic.

Using Google Cloud's Sentiment and Video APIs to detect the best moments of live events and create highlights automagically.

Check out some samples!

Google's Pixel Event: 1 hr 7 minutes to 22 minutes.

Innovating to Zero! | Bill Gates: 29 minutes to 8 minutes.

What's under the hood

  • Sentiment Analysis: What's being said? What's being said that's interesting, important?
  • Cloud Video Intelligence: How is the scene changing? When do we see something new, different?
  • Audio Analytics: What gets the crowd going? When do we hear applause, laughter?
  • (TODO) Realtime Response: When do the comments go crazy? What keywords trended on Twitter?

What's on top

  • YouTube Scraping: Mapping closed captions for rapid speech to text.
  • Parameter Optimization: Ability to tune the extent of highlights by adjusting block sizes, sentiment threshold and confidence intervals.
  • Synchronization: Blending audio sentiment and video intelligence results to create a smooth, wholesome scene.
  • A/V: PHP scripting with ffmpeg to cut and combine your highlight reel.
